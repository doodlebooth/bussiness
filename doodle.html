Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Nature of Our Service

SnapBooth Studio primarily provides an on-location photobooth kiosk experience where physical prints are dispensed directly at the kiosk immediately after your session. Most of our offerings are delivered in person at the point of use.

2. Physical Print Delivery

Prints are generated and dispensed at the kiosk within 30–60 seconds of session completion. No shipping is required for standard kiosk-based print orders.

3. Digital Delivery

Digital copies of your photos (where the option is available) are shared via:

  • WhatsApp or Email — delivered within 5 minutes of session completion.
  • QR Code Download — available immediately on screen after the session.

Please ensure your contact details are entered correctly at the kiosk for digital delivery.

Cancellation & Refunds

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Kiosk Session Payments

Payments made at our photobooth kiosks are for instant, on-demand services. Once a session is initiated and payment is processed, the service is considered rendered and the transaction is non-refundable under standard conditions.

2. Technical Failure Refunds

If a session fails due to a technical malfunction on our end (e.g., printer error, kiosk crash before print delivery), you are eligible for a full refund. To claim this:

  • Note the kiosk ID and session time displayed on screen.
  • Contact us at support@snapboothstudio.in within 48 hours.
  • Provide your payment reference or UPI transaction ID.
  • Refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment source.

3. Event Package Cancellations

For pre-booked event packages (weddings, corporate events, etc.):

  • Cancelled 15+ days before event: Full refund of advance payment.
  • Cancelled 7–14 days before event: 50% refund of advance payment.
  • Cancelled within 7 days of event: No refund applicable.
  • Rescheduling (subject to availability) is allowed once at no extra charge if requested at least 7 days prior.

4. Merchandise / Product Orders

For personalised photo merchandise:

  • Cancellations are accepted within 24 hours of placing the order, provided production has not started.
  • Once production begins, cancellations are not accepted.
  • Damaged or incorrect items will be replaced at no charge — report within 48 hours of delivery with a photo.

5. Refund Process

All approved refunds are processed to the original payment method. Credit/Debit card refunds may take 5–10 business days depending on your bank. UPI refunds typically appear within 2–3 business days.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

  • User dissatisfaction with the aesthetics of a successfully printed photo.
  • Incorrect poses or expressions captured during the session.
  • Failure to collect prints from the kiosk after successful dispensing.
  • Technical issues caused by the user's own device (for digital delivery).

7. Contact for Refund Requests

Email us at refunds@snapboothstudio.in with your transaction ID, session details, and the reason for your request. Our team will review and respond within 2 business days.
